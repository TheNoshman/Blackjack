Blackjack

Instructions

1. Attempt to beat the dealer by getting a total card count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.
2. In this simplified version, ace is worth 1. Face cards are 10 and any other card is its number value.
3. You can either 'hit' to take a random card from the pack to add to your total or 'stick' to keep your current hand and continue the game.
4. Try and get closer to 21 than the dealer!
